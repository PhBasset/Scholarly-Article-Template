Introduction

Triboelectric energy harvesters typically generate high AC open-circuit voltage of several hundred volts peak, with relatively low short-circuit current of a few tens of µA peak maximum . On the other side, most applications request input DC voltages around a few volts with associated DC current in the mA range, in order to provide a minimum of mean power of 100 µW, like for a Bluetooth Low Energy communication for instance. The conditioning electronics of the TENG is therefore an important subject and has recently attracted a lot of attention.
