Introduction

Triboelectric energy harvesters typically generate high AC open-circuit voltage of several hundred volts peak, with realtively low short-circuit current from a few to tens of µA peak. On the other side, most applications request input DC voltages around a few volts with associated DC current in the mA range, in order to provide a minimum of mean power of 100 µW, like for a Bluetooth Low Energy communication for instance. Then, the conditioning electronic of TENG is of important matter and have generate a lot of attention recently.
